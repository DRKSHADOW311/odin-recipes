Spaghetti Bolognese Recipe

Description

Spaghetti Bolognese is a traditional italian recipe that has been enjoyed over the world. It is a simple recipe that requires little cooking skills and tastes delicious.

This cheesy and saucy recipe will serve 2 adults and can be turned vegitarian by using beans and vegetables as a substitute for the mince.

Ingredients

  • 500g Mince (chicken, beef, or lamb)
  • 1 x Packet of Spaghetti
  • 1 x Can of Diced Tomato
  • 1/2 Cup water
  • 1 x Jar of Bolognese Sauce
  • 1 x White Onion
  • 1 x Garlic Clove
  • 2 x Tablespoon Salt

Steps

  1. Fry off and strain excess fat from mince while breaking it up on medium heat into a paper towel
  2. Peel and dice onion and crush garlic cloves, add olive oil to the saucepan, then add onion and garlic
  3. Add Diced Tomato can, bolognese Sauce and 1/2 cup of water to the mix and stir well
  4. Add a tablespoon of salt to pot and fill with water. Bring this pot to boil, then add the spaghetti
  5. Add a tablespoon of salt to the saucepan mixture and when the pasta is al dente, strain it
  6. Add the pasta to the saucepan and serve on plates or bowls with garnish (chives or clovers)
  7. Enjoy!
