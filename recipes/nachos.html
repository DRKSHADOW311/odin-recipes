Nachos Recipe

Description

Nachos is a delicious traditional mexican meal made using tortilla chips and either chicken, beef or lamb. However other meats are also acceptable. Nachos can range in spice from mild to hot as the cook chooses. This cheesy delight is bound to make your mouth salivate from the smell alone.

This particular recipe serves two adults, however more beans, mince and spice mix can be added to serve more. A vegetarian option is also available by simply substituting more beans in place of the mince.

Ingredients

  • 1 x Bag of Corn Chips
  • 500g Mince (chicken, beef, or lamb)
  • 1 x Salsa (spice is personal preference)
  • 1 x Satchet of Mexican Spice Mix
  • 1 x Can of Mexican Bean Mix (can use other types if preferred)
  • 1 x Tomato
  • 1/4 of Iceburg Lettuce
  • Guacamole
  • 1 x Small Bag of Shredded Cheese
  • Olive Oil

Steps

  1. Add olive oil into a saucepan and heat on medium heat
  2. Fry off and split up the mince in the saucepan until brown
  3. Use a strainer to strain the excess fat into a paper towel
  4. Add salsa, 1/2 cup of water, mexican spice mix and mexican bean mix to the mince
  5. Stir the mince until mixed thoroughly
  6. Simmer mixture for 15 - 20 minutes
  7. While simmering, cut up the tomato and lettuce and place these on a small plate with guacamole
  8. Open bag of corn chips onto a large plate and sprinkle shredded cheese over the plate
  9. Place plate in oven at 180 Degrees fan forced for 5 mins or in the microwave for 1 min
  10. Add the cooked mince mix over the cheese, ensuring an even spread
  11. Sprinkle more shredded cheese over the mince mix and serve the nachos with the side salad plate
  12. Enjoy!
