Caesar Salad Recipe

Description

Caesar salad is a lightweight healthy food that is good to eat for breakfast, lunch or dinner. Caesar can be customised to the preferrences of the cook as there are many styles.

This recipe will be a basic caesar salad, however it can be vegetarian if desired.

Ingredients

  • 1/2 Iceburg Lettuce
  • 1 x Pack of Croutons
  • Cheese - Parmesan (up to cooks preference)
  • 1 x Satchet of Caesar Dressing
  • 1 x Chicken Breast
  • 2 x Pieces of Bacon
  • Olive Oil

Steps

  1. Cook the bacon in a pan with some olive oil and set aside on paper towel to absorb the fats
  2. Cut the chicken into small pieces and cook these pieces in the same pan used for the bacon
  3. Cut up the lettuce and add this to a bowl, with the croutons and cheese
  4. Add the bacon and chicken to the bowl
  5. Add the caesar dressing over the salad and mix this well
  6. Enjoy!
